What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites visited by the user send to their device (computer, smartphone, or tablet), where they are stored and then sent back to the same sites during subsequent visits. Cookies are used to ensure the proper functioning of the site and to improve the browsing experience.

Types of cookies used

The website Azienda Agricola Cactus Gioielli uses only technical cookies that are strictly necessary for the functioning of web pages.

  • Session cookies: essential to allow navigation and the use of the website; they are automatically deleted when the browser is closed.
  • Preference cookies (if present): store any user preferences, such as cookie acceptance or site language.

No profiling cookies or third-party cookies are used. No personal data or user information is collected or shared for profiling or advertising purposes.

Cookie management

The user can manage, limit, or delete cookies through their browser settings. Below are links to support pages for the main browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Disabling or deleting technical cookies may make some site features unavailable or function improperly.
