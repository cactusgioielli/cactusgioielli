WHO WE ARE

We are a farm specializing in the cultivation and distribution of succulent plants. Our mission is to provide high-quality products, grown with passion and respect for the environment.

OUR HEADQUARTERS

Our headquarters is the beating heart of Azienda Agricola Cactus Gioielli. Here, every plant is grown with dedication and care, using natural methods that respect its growth cycle. Every day, we work with commitment to deliver healthy, strong, and high-quality plants.

TIMELINE

1990

The "Azienda Agricola Cactus" is born, founded by the Gioielli family with the aim of bringing their passion for succulents throughout Campania.

2005

Production expansion and introduction of new succulent varieties, expanding the customer base beyond the region.

2020

Innovation in cultivation processes with sustainable techniques and attention to the environment.

2025

A new generation in the company, digitalization and openness to online sites and social media.
